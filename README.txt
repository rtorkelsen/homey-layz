Bring your Bestway Lay-Z-Spa hot tub and pool filter pump into Homey and control them from anywhere. Set the water temperature, schedule the massage jets, manage the filter and lock the control panel – all through Flows or the Homey app. Compatible with Bestway spas sold under various brand names including Lay-Z, SaluSpa, Coleman, Aqua Spa, Biltema and more.

The app supports three device types: V01 spa models and standalone pool filter pumps connect via your Bestway Smart Hub account, while newer V02 spa models connect via a share code from the SmartHub Connect app. Flows let you heat the spa before you arrive home, send a notification when the target temperature is reached, or alert you when the filter cartridge needs replacing.
